  1. Click the Appearance tab. Notice that there are buttons across the top of the tab for various categories of options that you can set. Remember that many options are already selected for you.


  2. Click Titles. In the Title box, type Average Returns by Region.


Click to view the full window.


  1. Click the Advanced tab. By default, the statistic that is used to calculate the bar is Sum. Click the drop-down box and select Mean.

  2. Select the Show statistics next to bar check box if it is not already selected.

  3. Select the Additional statistical value to show check box, then click the drop-down list and select Frequency.


Click to view the full window.
