    2. AnalysisDescriptiveOne-Way Frequencies.

  2. In your report, you want to determine the frequency and percent for product categories and supplier companies. On the Columns tab, assign CategoryName and CompanyNameLong to the Analysis variables role. A separate one-way frequency table will be created for each of these two variables.
