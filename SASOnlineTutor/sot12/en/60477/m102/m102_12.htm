Creating List Reports (continued) Start the List Data task and assign variables to roles.
  1. In the Project window, select ProductSales to make it active. Select AnalysisDescriptiveList Data.

  2. You want your report to show orders and profit for each product. So, on the Columns tab, assign ProductName, RetailOrders, and OrderProfit to the List variables role. These are the variables that will be listed in the report.

  3. You also want to group products by category in your report. Assign CategoryName to the Group table by role and to the Identifying label role. These roles specify that the data will be grouped by CategoryName and that the value of CategoryName will appear only once for each group.

  4. Finally, you want to summarize orders and profit in your report. Assign RetailOrders and OrderProfit to the Total by role. A total will be generated for each of these variables. Because CategoryName is in the Group table by role, a total is generated for each category and for all values in the table.
