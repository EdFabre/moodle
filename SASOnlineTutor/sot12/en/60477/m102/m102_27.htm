Creating Summary Reports (continued) Arrange variables and select statistics.
  1. Click the Table tab.


  2. In the Available variables list, select CategoryName, drag it to the row box in the Preview area, and drop it.

Click to view the full window.

  1. Drag RetailOrders to the right border of the N box and drop it. Notice that Sum is automatically used as the statistic for the column.

    If you drop a variable in the wrong location, you can click the Undo last drop button ( ) or drag and drop the variable back in the Available variables list.

Click to view the full window.

  1. Drag OrderProfit to the right border of the RetailOrders box and drop it.

Click to view the full window.

  1. For OrderProfit you want to calculate the sum, mean, and percent. In the Available statistics box, select Mean and drag it to the right border of Sum box under OrderProfit. Drop it when the arrow points toward the Sum box.

Click to view the full window.

  1. In the Available statistics box, select ColPctSum and and drag it to the right border of the Mean box. Drop it when the arrow points toward the Mean box.

Click to view the full window.

  1. You also want to display statistics for all rows as well as for individual categories. In the Available variables list, select ALL and drag and drop it under CategoryName to create another row for totals. In the row position, the special ALL variable calculates statistics across all rows.
