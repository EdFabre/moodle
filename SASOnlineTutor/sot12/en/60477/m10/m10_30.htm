Summarizing Groups of Data (continued)
Write a clause to group results by the values of the column ID from the Clinic.Admit table. (End the clause with a semicolon.) 

proc sql;
   select admit.id,fee,total,balancedue,
          sum(total-balancedue) as TotalPaid
      from clinic.admit,clinic.insure
      where admit.id=insure.id
