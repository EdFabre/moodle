SAS Enterprise Guide Code Editor: Features

  1. To select text, you click and hold down the left mouse button, drag the mouse pointer to the end of the text you want to select, and release the left mouse button.

  2. If you click within the margin to select text, you select the entire line.

  3. Notice that text is color-coded. For example, keywords for DATA and PROC steps are dark blue and bold, keywords for statements within DATA and PROC steps are blue, and quoted text is magenta. If you misspell keywords or leave out a quotation mark, you can see the error!

  4. When you press the Enter key, you automatically indent the next line by the amount of space that the current line is indented. You can also indent using the Tab key.

  5. To view the high-level flow of your SAS program or see each detailed statement, you can click the minus sign in the margin to collapse a section or click the plus sign to expand a section.

  6. To create a line marker for easily accessing a line of code, you can bookmark a line in your program by pressing Ctrl+F2. A vertical rectangle in the margin indicates that the line is bookmarked. You can remove the bookmark by pressing Ctrl+F2 again.
