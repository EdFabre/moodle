Testing Your Knowledge

A comprehensive quiz at the end of each lesson helps you to evaluate your understanding of the lesson's content. After you answer the multiple choice questions and click Score My Quiz, a new window opens which contains your score, the full text of each question, the correct answer, and an explanation that includes links back to the topic material.

If your score is 80% or better, you have the option of marking the lesson complete. If you complete a lesson as part of a course, a check is displayed next to the lesson title on the Learning Path page.
