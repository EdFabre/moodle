Distribution Analysis (continued) Select distribution and plot options.

In this task, you select the type of distribution and plots you want to generate.

  1. Click the Distributions tab. Select Normal.

    SAS Enterprise Guide automatically generates statistical tests for normality, although it is not generally recommended that you rely solely on the statistical normality tests. You should also examine the normality assumption graphically.

  2. Click the Plots tab. Select Histogram and Probability plot.
