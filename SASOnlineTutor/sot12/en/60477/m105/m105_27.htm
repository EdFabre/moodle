Simple Linear Regression (continued) Explore model and statistics options.

In this task, you take a look at the model and statistics options for the analysis.

  1. Click the Model tab. For model selection procedures, such as stepwise regression, several options are available. For now, leave the default option, Full model fitted (no selection).

    In addition to stepwise options, model terms can be specified in this window for multiple regression analysis. Because you are running a simple linear regression, leave these options at their defaults for this analysis.

  2. Click the Statistics tab. A variety of options are available for evaluating relationships among multiple predictor variables and several diagnostic tests for assumptions of multiple regression analyses. For this analysis, do not select any options.
