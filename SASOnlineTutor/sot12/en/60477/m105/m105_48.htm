Analysis of Covariance (continued) Specify the plot appearance.

In this task, you specify the appearance of the plot you are generating.

  1. Click the Appearance tab.

  2. Be sure that the Plots button at the top is selected. For each level of the variable Type (A through E) listed in the Columns to plot box, change the interpolation method to Regression . By default, a unique color is automatically selected for each line.


Click to view the full window.


  1. Click Finish.
