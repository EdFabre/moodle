Two-Way Analysis of Variance (continued) Build the model.

In this task, you build the model for the analysis.

  1. Click the Model Builder tab. The model builder enables you to specify the terms in the model by adding or removing hierarchical effects. The main effects are included in the model by default.

  2. In the left panel, select Additive and shift-click (hold down the Shift key while clicking the mouse button) Brand. Click Factorial to add the two-way interaction term to the model.
