Control Charts for Attribute Data (continued) Edit the task and run the analysis.

In this task, you edit the task and then you run the analysis again.

  1. In the Project window, double click NP chart to open the task.

  2. On the Columns tab, assign the variable training to the Group analysis by variable.


Click to view the full window.


  1. Click Finish. In the message window, click No. A second set of results will be created.

  2. Look at the results. Now that the variables have been grouped by training level, it appears that the two processes are in control. Notice that the control limits are different for the two groups: the experts have tighter control limits and a smaller average number of defective parts than the novices.
