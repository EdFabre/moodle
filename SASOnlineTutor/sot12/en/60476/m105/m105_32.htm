The p-value (Pr>|t|) is small, and we conclude that the F-statistic for Humidity is statistically significant. Knowing the humidity allows you to predict strength significantly better than knowing the mean alone. The R-square value of .5511 indicates that humidity accounts for about 55% of the total variability in strength.

The estimate of the slope is –0.44827. In other words, a 1-unit increase in humidity produces an expected ½-unit decrease (approximately) in strength for the range of values studied.
