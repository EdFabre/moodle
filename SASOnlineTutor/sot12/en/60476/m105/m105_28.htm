Simple Linear Regression (continued) Set plot options.

To evaluate assumptions of linear regression, plots can be very valuable. In this task, you select options for the plot you want to create.

  1. Click the Plots tab. Notice that there are three buttons at the top of the tab. Be sure that Predicted is selected. To create a plot of Strength by Humidity, select Observed vs independents.


Click to view the full window.


  1. Click Residual. To create a plot of the predicted values vs. the residuals (error), select Ordinary vs predicted Y.


Click to view the full window.


  1. Click Influence. To examine the influence of individual observations, select DFFITS vs predicted Y.


Click to view the full window.
