Assigning Descriptive Labels

If you completed the lesson , you learned to use LABEL statements to assign descriptive text to the variables in your procedure output, as shown below. The LABEL statement has the same effect in SAS/GRAPH output. The LABEL statement below assigns labels to the variables BalanceDue and Company.

 

     proc gchart data=clinic.insure;
        vbar company / type=sum sumvar=balancedue
             patternid=midpoint;
        where company in
              ('A&R','ACME','RURITAN');
        label balancedue='Balance Due'
              company='Insurance Co. Billed';
     run;
