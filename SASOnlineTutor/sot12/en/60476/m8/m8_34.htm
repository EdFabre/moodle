Creating Interactive Graphs (continued)

When you submit the completed program, you create the graph shown below. (Colors and other features may vary depending on what settings are in effect.) 

     ods listing close;
     ods html body='barchart.htm';
     goptions device=activex xpixels=400 ypixels=300;
     proc gchart data=sasuser.heart;
        hbar3d shock / sumvar=arterial type=mean;
     run;
     quit;
     ods html close;





Changing the Graph's Appearance

When the graph is displayed, you can right-click areas of the graph and select items from a context-sensitive menu. In bar charts, you can change features of the bars, the axes, the legend, or the entire graph.

For example, right-clicking the chart and selecting Graph Properties displays the Properties window for the graph:





From this window, you can change properties for the entire graph. You can also click other tabs and change properties that are specific to the bars, axes, or legend. Advanced properties enable you to specify lighting, rotation, resizing, and image quality for the graph.

You can also double-click areas of the graph to display a window for setting graph properties.

You can return a graph to its original state at any time by clicking your browser's Refresh or Reload button. Within the interactive graph, you can right-click and select ViewReset View from the menu.
