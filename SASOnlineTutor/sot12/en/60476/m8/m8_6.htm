Specifying Titles and Footnotes

As with other SAS procedures, you can use TITLE and FOOTNOTE statements to enhance SAS/GRAPH output, as shown in the example below. 

     title1 'Total Revenue for Males';
     footnote1 'For March 1-15';
     proc gchart data=clinic.admit;
        hbar actlevel / sumvar=fee
             patternid=midpoint sum;
        where sex='M';
        label actlevel='Activity Level';
        format fee dollar5.;
     run;

This program produces a bar chart that has the title Total Revenue for Males and the footnote For March 1-15.
