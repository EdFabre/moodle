Assign a libref and check librefs in effect.

  1. Activate the Explorer window and view details about the libraries that are defined for the current SAS session.

  2. Write and submit a LIBNAME statement to assign the libref Mylib to a SAS library.

    If you don't know where any SAS files are stored, you can assign the libref to the host pathname shown for the Sasuser library.

    View the messages in the SAS log. Was the libref Mylib assigned successfully? In the Explorer window, view the librefs that are now in effect, including Mylib.

  3. Display the contents of Mylib.
