Specifying Results Formats (continued)

The Results Tab of the Preferences Window

You use the Preferences window to set the result format(s) that you prefer. Your preferences are saved until you modify them, and they apply to all output that you create.

To open this window in desktop operating environments, select Tools Options Preferences. Then click the Results tab. You can choose Listing, HTML, or both. You can also specify options for displaying and storing your results.


Results tab options may differ somewhat, depending on your operating environment. The example below is from the Windows operating environment.
