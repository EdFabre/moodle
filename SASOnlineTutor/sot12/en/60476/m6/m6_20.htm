Specifying Line Width

If you connect data points, you can also use the WIDTH= (or W=) option to specify the thickness of interpolated lines. By default, WIDTH=1.

The following SYMBOL statement specifies a smoothed line that is 2.5 times as wide as the default. 

     symbol1 value=square height=1 cm 
             interpol=spline w=2.5;
