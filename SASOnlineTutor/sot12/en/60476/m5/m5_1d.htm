Enhanced Tabular Report

You can enhance your tabular report with titles, footnotes, and labels. 

     proc tabulate data=clinic.admit;
        class sex;
        var height weight;
        table sex all,(height weight)*mean;
        title1 'Average Height and Weight for Males and Females';
        footnote1 'March 12, 2002;
        label sex='Sex of Patient'
              height='Height'
              weight='Weight';
        keylabel mean='Average'
                 all='All Patients';
     run;
