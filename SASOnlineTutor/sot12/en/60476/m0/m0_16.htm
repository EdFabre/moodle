Getting More Help

By clicking the Help menu on any lesson page, you can get useful information to help you learn:

  • Before You Begin opens key setup information about your training session, including steps to create sample data, check cookies in your browser, and resume lessons.

  • Troubleshooting Tips opens a list of possible issues you may encounter, along with ways to resolve them.

  • Glossary opens a glossary of SAS programming and software terms.

  • SAS Documentation opens a list of additional resources related to e-learning for SAS programming. Links to related SAS documentation also appear at the end of individual lessons.

  • Quick Tour opens this tour.
