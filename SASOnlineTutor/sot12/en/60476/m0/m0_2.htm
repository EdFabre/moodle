Learning Paths

The Learning Path page presents lessons (if you are taking a course) or topics (if you are taking a single lesson) in a sequence that is designed to steadily build your SAS programming skills. On the Learning Path page, click any title to open the lesson or topic.

If you are taking a course, the Learning Path page also gives you a visual indication of which lessons you have successfully completed. Once you complete a lesson and score at least 80% on the lesson quiz, you have the option of marking the lesson complete. On the Learning Path page, a check appears next to each completed lesson.

When you successfully complete the quizzes in a lesson or course, you can print a certificate of completion.
