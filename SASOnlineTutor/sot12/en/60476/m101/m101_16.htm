Subsetting Data in a Query

At this point, you've created a report of inventory levels for all products. But suppose that you want to view only products that need to be reordered. To do so, you can subset your data by retrieving only rows that meet certain criteria, based on values of columns in the data. Selecting rows in the Query Builder is called setting a filter.



To determine whether a product must be reordered, you need to find out whether the number of units in stock has dropped below the reorder level without an order being placed. So, to modify your report, you need to set a filter to check for two conditions:

  • UnitsInStock is less than or equal to ReorderLevel
  • UnitsOnOrder is equal to zero.

When you create more than one filter on your data, you specify whether the relationship between the filters is AND or OR. In this case, both conditions are necessary, so the relationship is AND.
