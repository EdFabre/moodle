Joining Tables Manually and Replacing Values (continued) Add a column to compute profit.
  1. On the Select and Sort tab, click New.

  2. In the Alias box, type Profit.

  3. Click the Expression tab. To define Profit as the net profit for a product times the number of products sold, copy and paste the following expression into the expression box:

    (Products.UnitPrice - Orders.UPRICE) * Orders.QUANTITY
