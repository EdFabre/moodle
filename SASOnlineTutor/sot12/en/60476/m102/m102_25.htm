Creating Summary Reports

In the previous report, you computed frequency counts on your data. But for your next report, you need to summarize data within categories. For the board meeting, you need a summary report that shows the following for each product category:

  • the number of orders and products sold
  • the total and average profit for orders
  • the percentage of total sales.

A summary report displays descriptive statistics in tabular format for some or all of the columns in your data.






Using the Summary Tables task, you can customize many aspects of the report, such as the following:

  • table layout
  • statistics that are used
  • format of the variable values
  • headings for columns and rows
  • report titles and text in the box area.
