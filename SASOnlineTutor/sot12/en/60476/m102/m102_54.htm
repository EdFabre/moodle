Using Styles to Customize HTML Reports (continued) Change foreground and background colors for headings and data cells in a style.
  1. In your new style, the background colors for column headings, row column headings, and data cells are custom colors. You want to change the colors to match your company logo. In the Edit Style window, click the Text tab.

  2. You start by changing column headings. Click the Column 1 heading in the preview area. To specify the text color, click the Foreground arrow under Colors. Click the new custom color that you defined earlier.

  3. To specify the background color for headings, click the Background arrow under Colors. Click light gray (directly under black).

  4. Next you change row headings. Click the Row 1 heading in the preview area. Click the Foreground arrow under Colors. Click the new custom color that you defined earlier.

  5. Click the Background arrow under Colors. Click light gray (directly under black).

  6. Finally, you change the background color for data cells. Click the Data (Num) cell under the Column 2 heading. Click the Background arrow under Colors. Click white (at the far right of the first row). Repeat this for the Data (Num) cell under the Column 3 heading.

Click to view the full window.

Next, you save the changes you have made to the new style.
