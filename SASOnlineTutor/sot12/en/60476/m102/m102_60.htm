Creating and Applying Custom Formats (continued) Assign labels and values for the format.
  1. Click the Define Format tab. The top area on the right side lists the labels and ranges after they are defined. You create the labels and ranges in the Label definition area.

  2. Click New Label. In the Label definition area, type Domestic in the Label box.

  3. Click New Range. Under Type, click the drop-down arrow and select Discrete. In the Values box, type USA.

Click to view the full window.

  1. Click New Label and type International in the Label box.

  2. Click New Range. Under Type, select Discrete from the drop-down list. Under Values, click the drop-down list and select All other values.

Click to view the full window.
