Creating Summary Reports (continued) Modify the table and rerun the report.
  1. In the Project window, double-click the Summary Tables task to open it.

  2. Click the Table tab.

  3. For your modified report, you want to categorize your data by supplier rather than by product category. The variable CompanyNameLong contains the name of the supplier. So, in the Preview area, select CategoryName and drag it back to the Available variables list. Then, drag and drop CompanyNameLong onto the row above ALL.

Click to view the full window.

  1. In the Preview area, right-click CompanyNameLong and select Heading Properties. In the Label box, type Company. Click OK.

Click to view the full window.

  1. Click the Titles tab. In the Text box, change Category to Company.

Click to view the full window.

  1. Click Finish. When you are prompted to overwrite the previous results, click No.

  2. View the report. Now the statistics are computed for suppliers rather than for product categories.

Click to view the report.
